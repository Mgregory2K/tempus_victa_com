# Tempus Victa // Cognitive OS

**Operational Status: v3.1.0 (Digital Twin Genesis)**

Tempus Victa is a local-first cognitive operating system. We have now initiated the **Digital Twin (Twin+)** phase, moving from a standard assistant to a personal cognitive model that learns and replicates the user's identity.

## 🧠 Twin+ Doctrine: The Mind vs. The Mouth
Twin+ is the app's continuous behavioral substrate. It decouples the "Mouth" (Language Engine) from the "Mind" (Cognitive Identity).
- **Layered Memory**: Stratifies information into Working, Episodic, and Semantic layers for human-like recall.
- **Lexicon Mirroring**: Learns Michael's specific vocabulary, tone, and digital dialect in real-time.
- **Identity Graph**: Stores a vector-based model of user preferences, risk tolerance, and decision-making patterns.
- **Sovereign Efficiency**: All modeling happens locally on the device to ensure privacy and low latency.

## 🛰️ The Bridge: Strategic Cockpit
The Bridge is the expression layer of your digital twin. It synthesizes data from all cognitive modules:
- **Interactive Metrics**: Deep-link navigation to all functional modules.
- **Temporal Timeline**: Live feed of unified Google Calendars.
- **Latest Triumph**: Real-time momentum tracking through persistence.

## 🎙️ Universal Ingestion (The Record Button)
A floating, visual-frequency monitored record button allowing for nonlinear input:
- **"Cork it"**: Routes thoughts to Spatial Memory.
- **"Task"**: Creates high-priority objectives.
- **"I wish..."**: Routes feedback to the Admin Wish Ledger.

## 📡 Sovereign Cloud Sync (Mothership)
Your entire OS state—including the **Twin+ Identity Graph** and API keys—is synchronized via a private vault in your Google Drive (AppData). This ensures your cognitive twin follows your identity across any hardware surface without requiring massive server storage.
