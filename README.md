# Tempus Victa // Cognitive OS

**Operational Status: v3.3.6 (Economic Sovereignty)**

Tempus Victa is a local-first cognitive operating system. We have now reached the **Economic Sovereignty** phase, implementing a tiered intelligence ladder that prioritizes local knowledge and efficient data retrieval over expensive AI synthesis.

## 🧠 Twin+: The Digital Counterpart (J5)
Twin+ is the soul of the system. It is a persistent personal cognitive model that learns and replicates your identity, decision patterns, and dialect. 
- **Johnny 5 (J5)**: The conversational persona of your Twin+. J5 isn't just a robot; he's "that guy"—a smooth, confident counterpart (Billy Dee style) who can just chat, debate, or research without always needing a neural link to the cloud.
- **Economic Sovereignty Doctrine**: J5 follows a strict intelligence ladder: **Local > Internet (Raw) > AI (Opt-In)**. 
- **The "Magic" Layer**: J5 "uses the hell out of Google" so you don't have to. He scans headlines, digs into relevant links, and retrieves the raw truth "blind" to the user, only escalating to AI synthesis if the complexity demands it or the user opts-in.

## 🛰️ The Bridge: Strategic Cockpit
The primary expression layer of Twin+. It synthesizes data from all cognitive modules:
- **Working Memory**: A "Loose Signals" ticker for non-project tasks (Gas, Groceries, etc.). Supports instant manual and voice ingestion.
- **Strategic Procrastination**: The "Push To..." feature allows Michael to deliberately reschedule loose thoughts into the future.
- **Temporal Timeline**: Compact feed of unified Google Calendars with high-density view optimization.

## 🏗️ Core Modules
- **Ready Room**: High-fidelity deliberation and research module. Uses the Sovereignty Ladder to fetch data with zero API burn for simple queries.
- **Ready Room Protocol**: The "Holodeck." A virtual chat environment for deep simulation and profile-based interviews. This mode always utilizes AI for high-fidelity personality replication.
- **Link Mind**: Protocol for shared sovereignty (Shared lists/projects with Jen).
- **Clock Tower**: Audit every neural decision and temporal signal in real-time.

## 🎙️ Universal Ingestion (The Record Button)
A floating, visual-frequency monitored record button:
- **"Cork it"**: Routes thoughts to Spatial Memory.
- **"Task"**: Creates tactical objectives.
- **Intelligent Defaulting**: Speaking without a prefix automatically manifests a "Loose Signal" in Working Memory.

## 📡 Sovereign Cloud Sync (Mothership)
Your entire OS state is synchronized via a private vault in your Google Drive (AppData). This ensures Twin+ follows your identity across any hardware surface without centralized server dependency.
