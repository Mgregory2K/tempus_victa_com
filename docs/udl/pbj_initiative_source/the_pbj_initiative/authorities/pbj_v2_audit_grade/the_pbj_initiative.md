The PBJ Initiative
(Portfolio Business Justification Initiative)
A governed, automatable idea-to-asset engine
This becomes the front door to every business, project, or acquisition idea — no exceptions.
________________________________________
1. What the PBJ Initiative Is (and Is Not)
It IS
•	A pre-build, pre-spend, pre-emotion decision engine
•	A Go / No-Go authority, not a brainstorming toy
•	A repeatable test that works for:
o	New microbusinesses
o	Spin-ups
o	Acquisitions (“buy vs build”)
o	Expansions
It is NOT
•	A pitch deck
•	A motivational exercise
•	A justification after you’ve already fallen in love with an idea
If it passes PBJ, we proceed with confidence.
If it fails PBJ, we stop without regret.
________________________________________
2. PBJ Core Test (The Non-Negotiables)
Every idea must satisfy all five to proceed:
1.	Ethical
o	Defensible publicly
o	No dark patterns
o	No dependency traps
2.	Repeatable
o	Can be used again for other businesses
o	Checklist-driven, not personality-driven
3.	Profitable
o	Money and time ROI
o	Founder time treated as capital
4.	Manageable
o	Can be delegated
o	Does not require constant executive babysitting
5.	Build vs Buy Clarity
o	Explicit answer to:
“Do we build this, buy this, or walk away?”
Fail any one → No-Go.
