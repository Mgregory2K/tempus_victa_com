# PBJ Charter — Portfolio Business Justification Initiative

## Purpose
The PBJ Initiative exists to ensure every business, project, or acquisition:
- Is ethical and defensible
- Is repeatable as a model
- Produces real profit (money + time)
- Is manageable and delegable
- Has explicit Build vs Buy clarity

No business proceeds without passing PBJ.

## Authority
This initiative operates under ai_government.zip as the governing authority.
PBJ does not override governance; it enforces it.

## Prime Rule
No sunk costs.
No emotional overrides.
No exceptions.

## PBJ v2.0 — Audit-Grade Upgrade (Effective: 2026-02-01)

### Epistemic Boundary Conditions
- PBJ is a **decision lens**, not a source of truth.
- **External evidence precedes scoring.** PBJ may weight evidence; PBJ may not invent it.
- Every scored axis must be backed by a **Claims Matrix** mapping claims → sources.

### Required Evidence Artifacts
- `00_source_register/` (source registers + manifest)
- `pbj_claims_matrix_template.csv` (instantiate per initiative)
- `pbj_bibliography_template.md` (instantiate per initiative)
- `pbj_audit_trail_template.md` (instantiate per initiative)
- `pbj_scoring_rubric.md` (controls scoring + confidence + assumption density)

### Evidence Sufficiency Gate (PASS / WARN / FAIL)
- **PASS:** All scored axes have Tier A/B sources; contrarian evidence recorded.
- **WARN:** One or more axes rely on Tier C or assumptions; verdict allowed with confidence penalty.
- **FAIL:** Major axes lack sources; **GO/NO-GO is not allowed** (Exploratory / Hold only).

### Non-Negotiable Output Rules
- Any GO/NO-GO verdict must include:
  - Scores (0–10), Confidence band, Assumption Density Score
  - Claims Matrix link/path
  - Bibliography (with access dates)
  - Contrarian evidence summary
