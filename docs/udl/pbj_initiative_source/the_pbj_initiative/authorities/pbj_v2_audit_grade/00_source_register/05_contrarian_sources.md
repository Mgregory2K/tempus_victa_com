# Contrarian / Disconfirming Sources Register

## Purpose
Prevent motivated reasoning by recording the best opposing evidence.

## What belongs here
- Studies that contradict your preferred conclusion
- Case studies where the opposite worked
- Market evidence showing adoption despite friction
