# PBJ v2.0 Scoring Rubric (Audit-Grade)

## Scores (0–10)
- **0–2:** structural failure (behavior/econ constraints dominate)
- **3–4:** severe friction (possible only with strong constraints)
- **5–6:** conditional viability (requires clear positioning and tradeoffs)
- **7–8:** strong viability within scope (niche or constrained market)
- **9–10:** broad scalable viability (rare; requires alignment + economics)

## Confidence Band (required)
Choose one:
- **High:** multiple Tier A/B sources agree; contrarian evidence addressed
- **Medium:** some disagreement or gaps; assumptions moderate
- **Low:** sparse sources; reliance on Tier C; assumptions heavy

## Assumption Density Score (ADS)
- **0–2 (Low):** assumptions minor; do not alter conclusion materially
- **3–5 (Medium):** assumptions affect parts of scoring; verdict conditional
- **6–10 (High):** assumptions dominate; verdict NOT allowed (HOLD)

## Evidence Sufficiency Gate
- **PASS:** all scored axes have at least one Tier A/B source
- **WARN:** one or more axes rely on Tier C or assumptions (confidence penalty)
- **FAIL:** major axes lack sources → HOLD only
